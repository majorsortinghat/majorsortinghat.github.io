Hello! Our names are Caspar, Zidane, and Priya, and we're taking CMS.633: Digital Humanities together at MIT. For our final project, we wanted to work on a problem that had some personal relevance. Zidane and Priya both struggled through MIT attempting to figured out what exactly they were interested, and we as a group want to encourage students entering MIT to get an idea of what majors they might be interested in outside of the common ones.

To do this, we created MIT Sorting Hat, an online questionnaire that can give students an idea of how close their personality might be to each major here specifically. Check out Our Dataset to see some more details about our process.
